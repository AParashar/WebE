29 Sept 2011:: New medicines launched and avaliable for heart patient.


The MediPharma Group of concerns is a well-known name in Pharmaceutical trading in Kerala. It was established in 1974 by the Late P.S Mani, the man who has been a leading light and a source of inspiration for the Pharmaceutical trade in India. The MediPharma Group has emerged to become the premier pharmaceutical group in Kerala by virtue of its excellent distribution network in the state and by it's spearheading the C&F movement of quite a few leading Indian and Multinational Pharmaceutical companies. Over the past three decades the MediPharma group has established a reputation of offering quality products of Indian and Multinational companies to the healthcare fraternity, which has been it's backbone till today.
